A little bit about myself

Hey, my name is Tegan Naicker and I'm originally from South Africa. My family moved to America when I was 2 years old and I have been living here ever since, mainly in the east coast. I come from a family of computer programmers and web developers. Both my sisters and father work with web development and coding as their jobs so, I guess you could say naturally, I gained an interest in coding as well. I learned to code a little bit on my own by creating simple games in different languages, such as very simple platformers and pong. I found the only real way to motivate myself to learn was by doing it in ways that I enjoyed. I don't really have much to say in terms of hobbies aside from playing the guitar. In my free time I mainly just play video games. Mainly fighters and Overwatch. Below, I will list 3 of the games I play the most.

Overwatch is probably my go to game when I want something to play for fun now. I started playing it since release, but took a bit of a break from playing it for 2 or 3 seasons. Just started playing it again a couple months ago and I now wonder why I even stopped playing it. Very fun FPS. Reminiscent of TF2.

Street Fighter V. One of the oldest fighters ever made. I started playing street fighter in 2012 with SFIV. Although I feel 5 is boring now and tend to play it a lot less than other fighters...

Guilty gear has quickly become my favorite fighting game. It's a more fast paced fighter, which I tend to like more. I've just started playing it more often a couple months ago, but I have played it casually since 2016. Beautiful art style and great information/tutorials for beginners.
